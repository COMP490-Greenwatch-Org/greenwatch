What is Greenwatch?

Greenwatch manages your lawncare, so you dont have to. Greenwatch uses existing security cameras to monitor your lawn, keeping track of any areas of concern and notifies you if theres any issues.

How can Greenwatch help me?

Greenwatch keeps track of shifting weather paterns and the condition of your lawn to make suggestions on how to keep your grass healthy.

What kinds of things can Greenwatch do?

Greenwatch is customizable, it can send notifications based on what matters to you, be it grass height or health. You can choose how and when you want to reveive notifications.
